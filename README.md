<h2 align="center">🔍 About Project Sentinel</h2>

**Project Sentinel** is an intelligent wearable bracelet engineered to enhance women's safety by providing real-time emergency support, stress detection, and powerful defense mechanisms — all within a sleek, wearable design.

---

<h2 align="center">✨ Key Features</h2>

- 📍 **SOS Alerts** — Instantly notify trusted contacts and local authorities with real-time location and health status.
- ❤️ **Heartbeat Monitoring** — Detect abnormal heartbeat patterns indicating stress, fear, or medical emergencies.
- 🎤 **Audio Recording** — Discreetly capture audio evidence during suspicious interactions.
- ⚡ **Integrated Stun Gun** — Built-in stun gun module for immediate self-defense during life-threatening situations.
- ☁️ **Cloud Backup** — Secure storage of heartbeat logs, audio recordings, and incident reports.
- 👩‍💼 **Elegant Design** — Ergonomically crafted for daily wear — lightweight, durable, and stylish.

<h2 align="center">⚙️ How It Works</h2>

1. **Idle Monitoring**: Sentinel tracks heartbeat continuously and remains ready in standby.
2. **First Tap**:  
   - Heartbeat is analyzed.  
   - Audio recording is automatically triggered.
3. **Second Tap**:  
   - Sends immediate SOS signals including location, health stats, and audio feed.
4. **Third Tap**:  
   - Activates the stun gun to defend against threats.
